Online Payment

Members paying for full KCC membership including Canoeing Ireland membership will be processed on the Canoeing Ireland portal. You will need to log in to Canoeing Ireland to pay for your membership.

KCC Only Membership (without Canoeing Ireland)

Only select payment via this option if you already have Canoeing Ireland membership through other means. E.g. through work or membership with another kayaking club. The KCC membership fee is €35. When selecting the payment link below, you will be redirected away from the KCC website to a secure payment page hosted by Stripe.


Pay Partial KCC Membership (€35).


Beginners Course

Please email the club directly to book a place on the beginners courses. We will be in touch on how to pay once your place on the course confirmed.
